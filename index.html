⚡ How It Works
AC
✔ VOLTAGE DETECTED!
  • 🔌 AC live wire generates an oscillating electric field around it
  • 📡 Antenna senses the field via capacitive coupling — no contact needed
  • 💡 LED glows RED = live wire (Danger!)  |  No glow = safe wire
🔵 Electric Field Detection
  • 🌐 AC current produces a pulsating EMF field radiating from the live wire
  • 🧲 Probe antenna connects to Pin 14 (clock input) of IC CD4017
  • ⚡ Detected EMF triggers the IC → LED flashes = live voltage confirmed
